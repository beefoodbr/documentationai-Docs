---
title: Order Failed to Process
description: Fix a failed BeeFood order.
---

## Symptoms

- Order won’t submit
- Error: "Order failed, please try again"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    Ensure your device is online and has a stable connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cart and Retry">
    Remove all items from the cart, add them again, and attempt checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment Method">
    Check if your card is valid and update if needed in your BeeFood profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

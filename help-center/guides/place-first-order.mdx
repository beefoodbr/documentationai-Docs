---
title: How to Place Your First Order
description: Quick steps to order food using BeeFood.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Restaurants">
    Log in and view available restaurants in your area from the homepage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Items">
    Pick your favorite dishes and add them to your cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout">
    Go to your cart, review your order, and tap **Checkout**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter shipping and payment info, then confirm your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>
